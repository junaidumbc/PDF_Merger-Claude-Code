About PDF Merger

A simple, fast, and private tool for combining PDF files.

How it works
  1. Upload or drag-and-drop your PDF files into the merge page.
  2. Reorder the files by dragging them into your preferred sequence.
  3. Click Merge PDFs to combine them into a single document.
  4. Download your merged PDF instantly.
Privacy

All processing happens on your local machine through the Flask server running on your computer. Your files are temporarily stored only during the merge operation and are deleted immediately after. No data is sent to any third-party service.
